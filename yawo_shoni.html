Switzerland

Switzerland, officially the Swiss Confederation, is a country in Central Europe known for its neutrality, mountainous landscapes, and high quality of life.

Switzerland is usually divided into three major geographical areas. From north to south, and in order of increasing size, these are the Jura, the Swiss Plateau, and the Swiss Alps. Due to its population density, the Plateau is the most important area in demographic and economic terms.
