History

Belgium, one of Europe’s earliest industrial powers, gainedindependence from the Netherlands in 1830 after centuries under Burgundian and later Habsburg rule, saw its medieval cities like Bruges flourish as major trade centers, and endured pivotal battles in both World War I and World War II, shaping its complex and influential place in European history.
